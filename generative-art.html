Generative Art

This project was my first attempt at Generative Art with JavaScript. I think it turned out great considering it was my first experience with the program, and I was able to take a few screenshots of some of the interesting designs I created while using it. While not the most complex code, there is an endless potential for the different designs that each user can create with just a few colored lines attached to anchor points. In order to access the Generative Art website, just click on the title above!
