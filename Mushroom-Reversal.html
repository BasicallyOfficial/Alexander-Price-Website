Mushroom Charcoal Reversal

This project was my attempt at a charcoal reversal. A charcoal reversal is the process of covering an illustration board in activated charcoal, and using an eraser to create an image out of the darkness. Through a long and grueling process that consumed many kneaded erasers and eraser pencils, I created the image of mushrooms in the dark, and was my first big project since I started attending SJSU.
