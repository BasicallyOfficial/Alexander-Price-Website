The Leviathan

This project was my first time making a 3D model after entering my first modelling class. All of my 3D work before this was self taught, and I this project was a massive step up in my ability to freehand Blender and make something that I am still proud of. This was also my first time 3D sculpting and experimenting with different types of lighting, which was a great start to improving my skills in 3D.
