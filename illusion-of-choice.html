The Illusion of Choice

This is my first game I have created, and its a browser-based choose your own adventure game that aims to tackle the grand concept of free will. Using the p5.js library "Scribble," I created an innocent, sketchbook style that contrasts with the over-arching themes of the game. My full Artist Statement is accessible via the titlescreen of the game itself. To play the game for yourself, just click on the title above!
