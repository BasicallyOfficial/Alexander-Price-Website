Flesh and Steel

This project is the first full 3D scene that I ever created, and is a big improvement since my previous work in Blender. Everything besides the floor and wall textures I found on Poly Haven was made from scratch based on what I had learned over the last few months including designs, models, textures, lighting, and the environment. This project is the culmination of everything I have been taught in Blender so far, and has opened up my abilities to tackle even greater projects in the future.
